Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

To place an order, browse products, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Fill in your shipping and payment details, then confirm your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Credit/Debit Cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Crypto (BTC, ETH, USDT), and Bank Transfer.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order is confirmed, you will receive a tracking number via email to track your shipment.

What is your return policy?

You can return products within 14 days of delivery. The items must be unused and in original packaging. Contact support for a return authorization.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we ship only within the USA. International shipping will be available soon.

How can I apply a coupon code?

On checkout, enter your coupon code in the gift card/coupon field and click Apply. If valid, your discount will be applied to your order summary.
